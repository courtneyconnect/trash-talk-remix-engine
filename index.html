Every remix below applies this 7-step framework, now upgraded with real performance data from Gary Vaynerchuk's own TikTok testing program.
1

Hook in 0–2 Seconds

Start mid-action. Cut the slow intro. Add a bold text overlay — Gary's team proved this acts like a subject line and stops the scroll immediately.

2

Use a Proven Format

Frame the clip as "3 Tips" (1.79x multiplier) or "5 Things" (1.33x). Or tie it to a National Day with the "Add Yours" feature for FYP explosion up to 94%.

3

Amplify the Emotional Peak

Find the single most emotional second — a kid's face, a reveal, a laugh — and zoom in or slow-mo it. Motivation and positivity are the #1 share-trigger topics.

4

Deploy the Share Mechanic

Use "The person who tagged you in this…" language for BSU posts — these hit 2,289 avg shares vs 709 for regular clips, even with just 1–3% FYP.

5

Gary Replies to Comments

6

Post at the Right Time

6PM and 7PM are the top-performing slots (812K and 747K avg). Best days: Monday, Thursday, Wednesday. Avoid Tuesday (340K avg — worst performing day).

7

Test 3 Versions

Post the same clip with 3 different titles across 3 days. Double down on the version that hits 65%+ FYP in the first hour — that's the algorithm's greenlight signal.
